About

The purpose of this web is to display the suggested books of the profile owner. The post will contain the book title, the writer, the genre, and a little bit of information about the book. This web is a social reading platform that allows users to post their own suggestions online. The benefits of this web esp to students is to gain more knowledge and improve their writing skills, vocabulary mastery, and speaking skills.
